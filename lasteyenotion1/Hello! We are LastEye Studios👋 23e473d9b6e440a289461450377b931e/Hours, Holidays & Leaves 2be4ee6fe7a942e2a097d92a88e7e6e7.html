Hours, Holidays & Leaves

Working Hours

We understand that everyone’s body clocks work differently. Some people are at their best early in the morning, and others are most productive later. We were hoping you could work at your best while making sure we get the benefits of bouncing ideas off each other.

We have core working hours of 12 pm to 6 pm, during which we need you to be available for any meetings and one-on-ones. We encourage you to not only step into work on time but leave on time to spend time with your family and friends. Considering it's an early-stage startup, we work from Monday to Saturday, keeping the last Saturday of the month off.

That said, we value the output you produce over the number of hours you are available on slack.

Holidays

How many days can I take off?

We want you to take at least 15 working days off per year. This is in addition to any public holidays. We have no upper limit but again, try to think of your team when booking your leave.

How do I book time off?

In the beginning, when we are still a small team, you can inform your reporting manager/team head (if you have one) or one of the co-founders (if you don’t) in person and then by email about your time off.

If the leave is planned, you should activate a vacation responder on your email and Google calendar, so everyone in the team knows you’re away. If you have to attend to an emergency, just drop a text to anyone of us.

Sick Leave

If you are feeling sick, please go home and let your manager know you are sick as soon as you can. Make sure you record your sick days on the LastEye Studios calendar when you can resume work.

Go to the doctor if you need to. We want to know if there is anything that we can do to help.

Health Coverage – we'll make sure that you have ample health insurance coverage so that you can be free of health worries.
